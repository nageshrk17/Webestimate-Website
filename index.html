your requirements

First step is to answer a set of multiple-choice questions about your specific needs. Is it a website or a mobile app? Is it e-commerce, custom or purely informational? The whole thing takes between 5 and 10 min is performed 100% online

our magic

Our algorithm is the result of a team of data scientists observing and time-tracking designers and programmers to breakdown their work into small units and identify the variables that influence the time they need to deliver a functioning application.

your estimate

With your requirements and the past data from similar projects, we’re able to accurately predict the time it should take to build your application. And since we also know the standard price per hour (factoring in country, level of experience etc.), we can ultimately estimate its price
